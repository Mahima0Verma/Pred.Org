About US

What Predulive Stands For ?

Predulive Edutech Foundation stands for inspiring, educating, and empowering youth, particularly in rural areas, by leveraging innovative training methods and project-based learning to create employment opportunities and bridge the gap in formal education, thereby nurturing curiosity and excellence.

Why Predulive ?

Predulive Edutech Foundation is dedicated to addressing rural youth unemployment by leveraging project-based learning, hands-on activities, and innovative manufacturing techniques. They aim to inspire curiosity, provide relevant skills, and create employment opportunities, ensuring youths are equipped for global standards.

Mission Of Predulive

Predulive Edutech Foundation envisions a future where every youth, regardless of their background, has access to quality education, skills development, and job opportunities. Our mission is to inspire, educate, and empower youth through innovative training methods, fostering entrepreneurship, and driving sustainable job creation.

Our Story

2019

Predulive embarked on a mission to empower underprivileged youth, offering them a platform for career guidance and job opportunities.

2020

Despite the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic, Predulive remained resilient, running social campaigns to provide employment opportunities in small towns and offering drone-based surveillance services to support public safety, recognized by UP Police administrators.

2021

Predulive expanded its impact by partnering with Model Gaon which was mentored by senior IAS officer Dr. Heera Lal who is well known as Dynamic DM for awareness campaigns across numerous villages, earning praise from influential leaders like Shri Amitabh Kant and Sherpa G2

2022

Predulive focused on skill development by organizing technical workshops in engineering colleges, aiming to create a skilled workforce aligned with its vision of empowering communities.

2023

Predulive continued its commitment to education and talent development by offering over 1000 free internships to college students in collaboration with startups, nurturing talent and fostering industry connections.

2024

Finally, Predulive is launching a world 1st integrated platform to support youth from guidance to funding for starting the business, empowering them to pursue entrepreneurial ventures and fostering a culture of innovation in small towns.
